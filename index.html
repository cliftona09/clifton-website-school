About

I am a full time instructor at the University of South Carolina. My academic interests include graph theory (in particular, the packing chromatic number, Ramsey theory, and domination) and mathematics education. Other interests include hiking, backpacking, fishing, cooking, spending time with my husband, and playing with our dog, Emmy.
